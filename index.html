02 · Story

My Story

I was born in Gulmi, a district in western Nepal, and spent most of my childhood in Tamghas, surrounded by the hills and close-knit community life that western Nepal is known for. Even as a young boy, I found myself drawn to the growing wave of technology reaching Nepal — a fascination that would go on to shape the entire course of my career. That early curiosity about how things worked eventually became a clear sense of direction, leading me toward the world of IT. In pursuit of higher education and greater career opportunities, I made the significant decision to leave home and move to Australia, a journey that opened the door to the professional path I'm on today.
